Have you ever wondered what the relationship between different health, and lifestyle factors are and how they can possibly contribute to your future wellness? Every year, about 800,000 people in the United States have a stroke - and about 1 out of 4 of those strokes are recurrent strokes. Anyone, including children, can have a stroke at any time, so it is important to take care of your health as a way to lower that risk. There are several factors that can increase your risk of stoke. Some of these factors are beyond your control such as your age, sex, and ethnicity. But there are many unhealthy habits, such as smoking, drinking, and not getting enough exercise, that you can increase your risk as well.
